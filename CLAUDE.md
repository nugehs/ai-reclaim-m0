## Project

**AI-Reclaim™** is a compliance-first, AI-enabled SaaS platform for IT recycling, asset disposition, and ESG reporting targeting regulated organisations (NHS trusts, banks, local authorities, enterprises, IT recycling operators).

**Core capabilities:** Digital chain-of-custody tracking, AI-enabled asset identification, secure audit logging, ESG impact reporting, certified destruction documentation.

**Owner:** A to Z IT Recycling Ltd (all IP under NDA/IP assignment)

**Author:** Oluwasegun Olumbe

## Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| ITAD | IT Asset Disposition — the process of disposing of obsolete IT equipment |
| Chain of Custody | Documented trail tracking asset handling from collection to final disposition |
| ESG | Environmental, Social, and Governance — sustainability reporting metrics |
| WEEE | Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment — EU directive on e-waste |
| Data Sanitisation | Secure erasure of data from storage devices (NIST 800-88 standards) |
| Certificate of Destruction | Legal document confirming secure disposal/recycling of assets |
| R2/e-Stewards | Industry certifications for responsible electronics recycling |
